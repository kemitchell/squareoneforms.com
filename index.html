Square One

fair terms. free forms. back to work.

Square One provides free, plain English legal templates for hiring employees and independent contractors in the United States. Anyone can use Square One forms to hire someone new, for free.

Looking to hire? Jump to instructions for employees or contractors.

Hire an Employee

  1. Download the offer letter template and fill out all the blanks marked with {curly braces}, then sign at the bottom under “employer.”
  2. Download the employment terms.
  3. Send the offer letter to your new employee, attaching the employment terms at the back. Ask them to sign and return to you.

Hire a Contractor

  1. Make sure that they’re really be a contractor. That’s a legal question, and not up to what your legal terms say. IRS publishes helpful guides. Check with your state labor agency’s website, too. When in doubt, ask a lawyer.
  2. Download the statement of work template and fill out all the blanks marked with {curly braces}, then sign at the bottom under “client”.
  3. Send the statement of work to your new contractor. Ask them to sign and return to you.
